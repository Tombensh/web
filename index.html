About us

LowerTone is a home-made bass guitar-shop, established in early 2019 by Tom Ben Sheffer.

It all started 10 years ago, on 2009. Tom, was an electric-guitar player studied in Hof-Hasharon regional school.
The end of the semester was near, and the staff searched for musicians among the students to assemble a band and play in the end of the semester ceremony.
Tom noticed that there are many guitar players, but no bass players. So he went on and tried it once. And in the next semester again.. and again...
Tom became a decent Bass player (the only bass player in school), and liked it!

Ever since, Tom has the passion for bass guitars. He played in many many bands and music-groups, performed on many stages and recorded some music too.
Ever since Tom volunteered for the army, he did not manage to keep on playing liked he used to do, but the passion for bass guitars kept occupying his dreams.

So! in order to keep on working on his desire, Tom opened the online bass-guitar shop - LowerTone.
